Pale-Remembrance · Coven of the Marrowgate

Blood Compact

— sealed in the year four hundred of the slow sun —
First Party · Petitioner
Reyl'an Vh'Aelir
Cousin-Second of House Vh'Aelir, late of Blackstone. Petitions safe passage and the silence of the long ledger.
⚝
Second Party · Holder
Lady Vesperia Mournfall
Elder of the Coven, keeper of the Twelfth Archive. Grants the silence — for a price.
Whereupon the parties do bind, in blood and ledger
  1. The First Party shall be afforded safe lodging within Coven precincts, his name struck from open archive for a tidal year.
  2. In return, the First Party shall surrender three readings of his blood at the discretion of the Second — taken within the Transition Window, witnessed by an unFormed scribe.
  3. The First Party may not seek shelter with any rival Coven, Pack, or independent shaman, save by writ of the Second Party.
  4. Upon the breaking of any clause herein, the long ledger shall reopen against the First, with compounding cost.
Ledger Trace Strengthiv of v · Heavy
Enforcement · A spirit-witness of the Mournfall line stands attendant to this compact. Should the First Party speak falsely under its presence, the spirit shall mark him with a Spirit-Scar visible to any Reader of Blood, and the compact shall be considered breached.
