Lunar · Tidal · The Ice-Blue Moon

Moon & Tide

— a fifteen-day cycle within a thirty-day month —
Tidal Month · Six Five-Day Stages
Stage A
Spring Surge
Days 1–5
Aligns with Full Moon. Coastal flood; rituals at full power.
Stage B
Ebbing
Days 6–10
Pack activity wanes. Trade caravans resume.
Stage C
Neap
Days 11–15
Quiet waters. Bloodcraft traces fade quickest.
Stage D
Slack
Days 16–20
Spirits drowsy. Negotiations favored.
Stage E
Rising
Days 21–25
Coven archives stir. Compacts hard to break.
Stage F
Crest
Days 26–30
Returns to Stage A. Alignment imminent.
Faction Modifiers
Packs
Full Moon (Day 8)
Forming thresholds halved. Hunt Courts in session. Pack cohesion at peak.
Covens
Stage A / Full Alignment
Blood-archives most readable. Pale-Remembrance rites observed.
Drow Autonomy
New Moon (Day 1, Day 15)
Web operates with reduced detection. Inquisition tribunals convene.
Shamans
Stage C · Neap
Ancestor contact strongest in still water. Birth and Forming rites scheduled.
Free State
Stage B / D · Slack
Caravan windows. Guild Tallies clear and trade negotiations finalize.
Hidden Circle
Full Moon + Stage A
Unraveling fractures most likely. Pact debts come due.
Alignment

Two lunar cycles fit within a single tidal month. The second Full Moon (Day 23 of the tidal month) does not coincide with Stage A — only the first does. This single alignment is the most dangerous night in Dusk: Pack, Coven, and Circle act in concert with the world's tide. Plans are made and unmade by it.
